Our Story

The Brand Journey

At Venom Guitars we have a dream to manufacture an all-Australian range of professional custom guitars for our customers. Our goal is to build a recognisable brand in the Australian music industry — one that earns respect from the ground up.

We want Venom Guitars to be recognised by grassroots and professional musicians alike, initially in Australia, and ultimately to become a globally recognised brand. We're a supportive force in the music industry, providing high-quality instruments customised to each player's exact specifications.

We use a range of Australian and international hardwoods and resins to create musical works of art — guitars that look as dangerous as they sound.

The Process

How We Build

01
Design & Consult

We work with you to nail the spec — body shape, tonewoods, pickups, hardware. Every build starts with a conversation.

02
Wood Selection

We source premium Australian hardwoods — Blackwood, Rosewood, Silky Oak — alongside international tone timbers for the perfect sonic foundation.

03
Craft & Carve

Hand-routed bodies, precision neck joints, bound fretboards. Each piece is shaped, sanded, and fitted by human hands. No shortcuts.

04
Finish & Set Up

Resin art pours, lacquer finishes, full professional set-up. Your guitar plays perfectly before it leaves the shop.
