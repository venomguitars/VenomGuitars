The People
Our Staff
Two mates. One mission. Deadly guitars.

Venom Guitars is headquartered in Sydney, NSW — a company born between two long-time friends who reconnected after years apart, combining their talents in woodworking, music, and business.

Co-Founders

The Founding Team

🎸
PLUMMO
Co-Founder & Director

Plummo is one half of the founding duo behind Venom Guitars — a lifelong musician and craftsperson who's been building and playing guitars for as long as most people can remember. His background sits squarely at the intersection of music and woodworking, giving him an instinctive sense of how an instrument needs to feel in the hands of a player, not just how it looks on paper.

As a Co-Founder and Director, Plummo drives the creative and technical direction of Venom's builds — from initial design concepts through to final setup. He believes that a guitar should be a genuine extension of the player, built to their exact requirements and tuned to their style. Every build decision reflects that philosophy.

HAZY
Co-Founder & Director

Hazy is the other half of the equation — and the person who brings the timber expertise, business acumen, and relentless drive to get things done. As the force behind Hazy's Wood, he has deep connections in the Australian hardwood space, giving Venom Guitars access to premium native timbers that simply aren't available through conventional channels.

As Co-Founder and Director, Hazy manages the supply chain, partner relationships, and operational backbone of the business. He's the person who makes sure the timber arrives, the build schedule is realistic, and the business runs the way it should. He also plays — which means he understands from the inside what a great guitar needs to feel like.

Origin Story
Two Old Mates, One New Mission

Plummo and Hazy are long-time friends who reconnected after a number of years apart. Both had built careers in their respective fields — music and woodworking, business and timber — but neither had stopped thinking about the idea of a truly premium, genuinely Australian guitar brand.

When they finally sat down together and put the idea on the table, it was obvious: they had exactly the combined skillset to make it happen. Venom Guitars was the result. Not a corporate venture, not an investor pitch — just two people who love music and craft, deciding to build something worth building.
