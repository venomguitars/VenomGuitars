The Inspiration

Australian Red-Bellied Black Snake

Pseudechis porphyriacus

The Australian Red-Bellied Black Snake is one of Australia's most iconic venomous snakes — known for its striking glossy black upper body contrasted by a vibrant crimson to pinkish underside. Commonly found along the east coast in wetlands, forests, and grasslands, this species is generally shy and prefers to avoid confrontation, though it will defend itself if threatened.

Despite its potent venom, bites are rarely fatal thanks to modern medical treatment. The red-bellied black snake plays an important ecological role, feeding on frogs, fish, small mammals, and other reptiles, helping maintain balance within its habitat. Its distinctive appearance and calm nature have made it a well-recognised and respected symbol of Australia's diverse wildlife.

Design Concept

Nature's Own Design

One of Australia's most iconic venomous snakes — the "Red Bellied Black" — is the concept behind this design series. It brings the aesthetics of a deeply black body and neck/headstock with the striking red front, replicating nature's own breathtaking design language.

The guitar is available in your choice of configurations. Standard being 6 String Electric and 4 String Bass. 7, 8 String electric and 5 String Bass available on request.
