Flagship Series

Red Belly Black

Available

Named after Australia's most iconic serpent, the Red Belly Black series is our flagship — bold, dangerous, and impossible to ignore. The body is carved from premium Australian Blackwood, with a hand-poured resin art finish featuring the black and red colourways of its namesake.

Hot single-coil pickups deliver that crisp, biting Aussie tone. Whether you're playing blues, rock, or going full noise, this guitar cuts through like a black snake through long grass.

Taipan Series

In Development

The Taipan — Australia's most venomous land snake — demands a guitar that lives up to the name. This series is built for the heavy hitter. Mahogany body for deep, resonant warmth. Twin high-output humbuckers for serious bite.

We're currently prototyping the Taipan series — testing different Mahogany-Maple combinations, refining the neck profile, and dialing in the pickup wind for maximum aggression without muddiness.

If you want early access or want to commission a pre-production Taipan, reach out now.

Future Series

Death Adder

Concept Stage

Short. Wide. Devastating. The Death Adder series concept takes a radical approach — a short-scale, wide-body guitar optimised for low tunings and baritone playing. Named after Australia's deadliest ambush predator.

This is still in concept stage, but if you're interested in a baritone or short-scale commission in this direction, we're open to discussion. Be part of shaping the series.

Interested in Influencing This Series?

We want player input on the Death Adder. Tell us what you want — specs, playability, price point. The people who reach out now will shape what this guitar becomes.
