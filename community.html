The Pack
Our Community
Grassroots players, builders, and tone chasers

Venom Guitars was built for musicians — and we mean that. From garage jammers to gigging pros, this community is for anyone who cares about playing great instruments built right here in Australia.

Why Community Matters

More Than Guitars

🎸
Players First

We started as players. We know what it's like to deal with a guitar that fights you. Everything we build is designed around the player experience — playability, tone, and feel over everything else.

🔧
Builders Welcome

Want to learn lutherie? We actively encourage it. Share your builds, ask questions, and get real feedback from people who've made the same mistakes and figured out how to fix them.

🇦🇺
Proudly Local

The Australian music scene is world-class and chronically underrepresented. We want to amplify local artists, support local gigs, and shine a light on the incredible talent in this country.
