Origin Story

Where It All Started

Venom Guitars was born from a simple but powerful idea — that Australia deserves its own world-class guitar brand. Not a badge-engineered import, not a cheap knockoff. A genuinely Australian instrument, built with care, using local materials, and reflecting the unique spirit of this country.

We started with a love of music, a workshop, and a stubborn refusal to settle for less than perfect. Every guitar we've built has pushed our craft further — better joinery, better finishes, better setups. The snake wrapped around that guitar in our logo isn't just a logo. It's a promise of something dangerous, beautiful, and alive.

Australia has some of the finest tonewoods in the world. Blackwood, Queensland Maple, Silky Oak — timbers with soul. Combined with international classics like Mahogany and Alder, our guitars sing with a character that's distinctly ours.

"The goal was never to make another guitar. The goal was to make the kind of guitar that makes a player stop, look down, and think — 'where has this been all my life?'"
— Venom Guitars Founder

The Journey

Milestones

🔥
2022
The Idea Strikes

The concept for Venom Guitars forms — an Australian custom guitar brand with a distinctive identity and uncompromising quality standard.

🪵
2023
First Builds Begin

Workshop established. First timber sourced. Prototype builds underway, testing designs, materials, and construction techniques.

🐍
2024
Red Belly Black Series Launch

Our flagship series launches — inspired by Australia's iconic Red Belly Black snake. Resin art bodies, hot pickups, and serious playability.

🌐
2025
Brand Goes Online

Venom Guitars launches across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook. Build videos, education content, and community growth.

⚡
2026+
What's Coming

New series in development. Expanded custom build program. Education partnerships. The snake is just getting warmed up.
